Here are some pictures of Nyahvn ("New Harbor" in Copenhagen) and Amalienborg (the Royal Family's palace also in Copenhagen).

1/5
Originally named Nyhavn "The New Harbor" by King Christian V as an alternative business port.
2/5
This is where the all of the Royal Family lives.
3/5
Fredrik's Kirke (Church) is the church of the Royal Family. This is where they go to get cristened, married, etc. Construction began in 1740 but was not completed until 1849.
4/5
This is my personal favorite Christmas dessert Æbleskriver. I explain this as a Danish pancake ball. They are served with your choice of jam and powdered sugar
5/5
I am a big kid so Legoland is one of my favorite places to go to when we go to Denmark. Many people don't know that Lego is a Danish company!
